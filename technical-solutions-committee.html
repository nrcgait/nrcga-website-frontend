Technical Solutions Committee

The Technical Solutions Committee evaluates and develops innovative technologies and methods to improve damage prevention practices across Nevada.

The Technical Solutions Committee focuses on identifying, evaluating, and promoting innovative technologies and methodologies that enhance damage prevention efforts. This committee brings together technical experts to explore solutions that improve locating accuracy, excavation safety, and overall system effectiveness.

This committee is also the primary steward of the NRCGA DIRT database.

Nevada DIRT

Key Responsibilities

  • ✓ Evaluate new technologies for damage prevention applications
  • ✓ Develop technical standards and best practices
  • ✓ Research and test innovative locating and excavation methods
  • ✓ Provide technical guidance to the NRCGA community
