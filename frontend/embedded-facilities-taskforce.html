Embedded Facilities Taskforce

Addresses challenges and solutions related to embedded facilities and infrastructure. This working group focuses on technical solutions for damage prevention.

The Embedded Facilities Taskforce is a technical working group focused on addressing the unique challenges associated with embedded facilities and infrastructure. This group brings together experts from across Nevada's damage prevention community to develop solutions and best practices for working with embedded utilities.
