Utility Locate Rodeo

Competition that showcases the locating profession and encourages best practices among utility locators

About the Competition

The Utility Locate Rodeo is an annual competition that brings together utility locators from across Nevada to demonstrate their skills, share knowledge, and celebrate excellence in damage prevention.

Participants compete in various challenges that test their expertise in identifying and marking underground utilities, their knowledge of best practices, and their accuracy. This event not only recognizes outstanding performance but also serves as an educational opportunity for the entire damage prevention community.

Event Highlights

Skills Competition

Locators demonstrate their expertise in identifying and marking underground utilities accurately and efficiently.

Best Practices Showcase

Participants share knowledge and techniques that promote safety and accuracy in utility locating.

Networking & Education

Connect with peers, learn from industry experts, and stay updated on the latest technologies and techniques.

Recognition & Awards

Outstanding performers are recognized for their excellence in utility locating and damage prevention.
