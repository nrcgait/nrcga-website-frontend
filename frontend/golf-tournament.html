NRCGA Programs

Golf Tournament Fundraiser

Proceeds support outreach, education, and damage prevention programs statewide.

The NRCGA Golf Tournament is an annual fundraising event that brings together members of Nevada's damage prevention community for a day of golf, networking, and fun. Proceeds from the tournament directly support NRCGA's outreach, education, and damage prevention programs across the state.

How Your Support Helps

  • ✓ Funds safety training programs for excavators and locators
  • ✓ Supports community outreach and public awareness campaigns
  • ✓ Enables recognition programs like the Silver Shovel Awards
  • ✓ Helps organize events like 811 Day and the Utility Locate Rodeo
