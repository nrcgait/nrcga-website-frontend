NRCGA Programs

811 Day (August 11)

Public awareness outreach to spread "Call 811 Before You Dig" across Nevada communities.

Every year on August 11 (8/11), NRCGA joins the national effort to raise awareness about the importance of calling 811 before digging. This annual event brings together stakeholders from across Nevada's damage prevention community to spread the message of safe digging practices.

Why 811 Day Matters

  • ✓ Raises public awareness about the importance of calling 811 before any digging project
  • ✓ Prevents utility damages that can cause injuries, service disruptions, and costly repairs
  • ✓ Builds a culture of safety across Nevada communities
  • ✓ Brings together stakeholders to celebrate damage prevention achievements

How We Celebrate

NRCGA organizes various activities and events throughout Nevada to mark 811 Day, including:

Community Events

Public outreach events at community centers, parks, and public spaces

Educational Workshops

Training sessions for homeowners, contractors, and excavators

Media Campaigns

Social media and traditional media outreach to reach wider audiences

Partnership Activities

Collaborations with utilities, contractors, and community organizations
