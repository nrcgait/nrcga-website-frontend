Operations

This group oversees how the One Call Center operates for Nevada and discusses potential changes that could impact its functionality.

The Operations working group plays a critical role in ensuring the effective operation of Nevada's One Call Center system. This group brings together stakeholders to discuss operational improvements, address challenges, and ensure that the 811 system serves Nevada's damage prevention community effectively.

Key Responsibilities

  • ✓ Oversee One Call Center operations and performance for Nevada
  • ✓ Discuss and evaluate potential changes that could impact system functionality
  • ✓ Address operational challenges and identify improvement opportunities
  • ✓ Coordinate with USA North 811 and other stakeholders on operational matters

How It Works

The Operations working group is made up of dedicated volunteers from across Nevada's damage prevention community. Members meet regularly to:

Review Operations

Monitor One Call Center performance and identify areas for improvement

Evaluate Changes

Assess proposed changes and their potential impact on system functionality

Problem Solving

Address operational challenges and develop solutions

Stakeholder Coordination

Work with utilities, excavators, and other stakeholders on operational matters
