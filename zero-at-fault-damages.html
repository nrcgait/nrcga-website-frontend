About the Program

The Zero At-Fault Damages recognition program celebrates excavators and contractors who have maintained zero at-fault damages, demonstrating exceptional commitment to safety and damage prevention practices.

This program highlights organizations that have built a safety-first culture and consistently follow best practices in damage prevention, protecting Nevada's underground infrastructure.

Companies achieving zero at-fault damages are recognized for their outstanding performance and commitment to the NRCGA's mission of promoting public safety and damage prevention across Nevada.
