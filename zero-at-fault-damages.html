Zero At-Fault Damages

The Zero At-Fault Damages program recognizes excavators and contractors who have achieved zero at-fault damages, demonstrating exceptional commitment to safety and damage prevention practices.

Thank you Excavators!

The Nevada Regional Common Ground Alliance extends our sincerest congratulations and appreciation to the excavators listed below for achieving a remarkable milestone. No at-fault utility damages throughout the year! This achievement not only reflects their dedication to maintaining the highest standards of safety and damage prevention, but also contributes significantly to making Nevada a safer place for everyone.

Our Silver Shovel Award is a testament to excellence in damage prevention, and these companies embody the spirit and criteria we seek in potential awardees.
