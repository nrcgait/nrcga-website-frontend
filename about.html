Our Mission

NRCGA is dedicated to promoting public safety and damage prevention across Nevada. We bring together excavators, utility operators, locators, and stakeholders to build a culture of safety through education, collaboration, and continuous improvement.

Every dig matters. Every call to 811 prevents potential damage, protects lives, and ensures the reliability of essential services. We're committed to building a safer Nevada where underground infrastructure is protected and respected.

Common Ground Alliance

NRCGA is a regional partner of the national Common Ground Alliance.

The Common Ground Alliance (CGA) is the national organization dedicated to preventing damage to underground utility infrastructure and protecting those who live and work near these important assets. CGA provides a forum where stakeholders can share information and perspectives and work together on all aspects of damage prevention issues.

As a regional partner, NRCGA aligns with CGA's mission and best practices while addressing the specific needs of Nevada's damage prevention community. We work together to promote safety, share knowledge, and advance damage prevention initiatives at both the regional and national levels.

Visit Common Ground Alliance

Public Utilities Commission of Nevada

NRCGA works closely with the Public Utilities Commission of Nevada to promote damage prevention.

The Public Utilities Commission of Nevada (PUCN) is the state regulatory agency responsible for overseeing utilities and ensuring public safety. The PUCN plays a vital role in damage prevention by regulating utility operations and enforcing excavation safety requirements in Nevada.

NRCGA collaborates with the PUCN to support damage prevention initiatives, provide education and training, and ensure that Nevada's excavation laws and regulations are effectively implemented. The PUCN also hosts NRCGA membership meetings at their offices in both Carson City and Las Vegas, facilitating statewide participation in damage prevention efforts.
